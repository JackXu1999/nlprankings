Text Analysis for Identifying Entities and their Mentions in Indian
Languages

Sobha L
AU-KBC Research Centre, Chennai, India

sobha@au-kbc.org

The talk deals with the analysis of text at syntactic-semantic level to identify a common feature set which can
work across various Indian languages for recognizing named entities and their mentions. The development of
corpora and the method adopted to develop each module is discussed. The talk includes the evaluation of the
common feature set using a statistical method which gives acceptable levels of recall and precision.
